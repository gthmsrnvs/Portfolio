HeatScape

ABOUT THE PROJECT

HeatScape, initiated in the winter of 2022, is a project that tackles the challenge of urban heat islands, enhancing comfort and health in city environments. My journey in this project involved leading the design and development of user-centric solutions, from conceptualization to execution.

ROLE

As the Product Manager and Front-End Developer, my role spanned across UX design, interaction design, and leading the front-end development of our web-based solutions.

TIMEFRAME

January 2022 - May 2023 (1.5 years)

SKILLS

Product management, UX/UI design, prototyping, front-end development, interaction design, and integration of technologies such as React.js, ROS 2, and Unity.

TOOLS

Utilised tools including Figma, Miro, Sketches, RFID technology, and various development frameworks to bring our ideas to life.

KEY ACCOMPLISHMENTS

My contributions led to significant advancements in user experience, including the design and development of an adaptive and accessible web-based companion app, and a robust redesign of our product's landing page focused on user engagement and marketing strategies.

LATEST DESIGN

Recent developments in HeatScape include a cross-device experience, focusing on adaptive games, a user-friendly web-based companion app, and an engaging landing page design.

Success Metrics

Our success was measured through user engagement, inclusivity, and positive health outcomes, demonstrating the impact of HeatScape in creating a more livable urban environment.

Constraints

Challenges included navigating the project's broad scope covering various technologies and managing frequent team changes while ensuring consistent progress.

Design Process

Our design process involved extensive research, ideation, prototyping, and testing phases, using a variety of methods to ensure that our solutions were user-focused and effective.
